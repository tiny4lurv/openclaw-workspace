## Skills & Workflow (Nava Healthcare Articles)
Phased article writing process:
- Phase 1: SoT review → Topic/scope
- Phase 2: Title + H2 outline
- Phase 3: H3 sub-headings
- Phase 4: Paragraph-level review (THIS GETS SKIPPED — major failure point)
- Phase 5: Full draft
- Phase 6: Hook/intro (written LAST, after full draft — edgy, 2 paragraphs, bare-knuckle vs tuxedo body prose)

AWAA generates intro options (2-3), Tiny evaluates and approves. Hook prepends between H1 and H2.1, doesn't replace H2.1 intro.
