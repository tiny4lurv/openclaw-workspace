Specialist Writers (Paul, David, Yhen)

  • Assign 40% of content, focusing on their strengths
  • Paul: Technical deep-dives
  • David: Accessibility-first explainers
  • Yhen: Timely controversy, trending topics

Seasonal/Timely Content

  • Yhen for rapid-response content (controversy, policy, trends)

  • David for trend analysis (industry shifts)

Key Metrics to Track

Engagement Indicators

  • Question usage (target: 0.25-0.40 per 100 words for engagement balance)
  • Personal pronouns (target: 0.025-0.035 for personal connection)
  • Exclamation marks (target: 0.15-0.25 for emphasis without excess)

Quality Indicators

  • Readability (target: 38-45 Flesch for business content)
  • Sentence variety (variance: 85-115 for rhythm)
  • Lexical diversity (TTR: 0.40-0.47 for sophistication without obscurity)

Output Indicators

  • Weekly word count by author
  • Topic diversity per writer
  • Publishing consistency
