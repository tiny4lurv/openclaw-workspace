### 1. Clear Authorial Fingerprints Exist

- **David Landau**: Most readable (Flesch 43.16), natural English patterns
- **Paul Sial**: Richest vocabulary (TTR 0.472), most complex prose
- **Yhen Villas**: Most engaging (highest questions), dynamic sentence rhythm
- **Sherry T.**: Best sentence variety, consistent professional tone
- **Tiny Manyonga**: Highest volume, comprehensive business coverage

### 2. Trade-offs Are Inevitable

- Higher vocabulary richness → lower readability
- More personal engagement → less formal tone
- More questions → less authoritative delivery
- Longer articles → higher word count but potentially lower completion rates

### 3. No Universal "Best" Style

The optimal style depends on:
- Target audience (technical vs. general)
- Content purpose (education vs. conversion)
- Platform requirements (LinkedIn vs. blog)
