## What is a SoT?

**SoT = Statement of the Article**

The SoT is the research document that precedes every piece of content. It is:
- The evidence layer (sourced claims, verified stats, structural arguments)
- The bones of the article — not prose, but argument architecture
- What Tiny feeds into the CustomGPT (Article Architect) to generate the annotated outline

The SoT is the part I need to learn how to produce autonomously for NavaAA.
