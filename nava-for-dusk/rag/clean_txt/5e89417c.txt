What Makes It Work Long-Term
We don’t just focus on the placement but what happens after. The employers who treat their international staff with respect, mentorship, and growth? They’re the ones who retain them long after the ~3 year contract ends.
With NurseSphere™, every candidate arrives with a green card in hand and a clear three-year work commitment already in place. You get a single, trusted partner for every stage:  sourcing, legal, and relocation. The process stays streamlined, compliant, and well-documented.
All candidates are fully licensed, vetted, and prepared to integrate into your workplace from day one.
Time to hire typically averages about two years from start to finish (dependent on I-140 processing timelines and embassy availability). While prevailing wage rates may allow you to pay less than local hires, we strongly recommend aligning pay with local staff, even if implemented after the first year, to build loyalty, increase retention, and ensure your nurses choose to stay well beyond the three-year agreement.
