Primary and Urgent Care Clinic Recruitment FAQs
Can you help reduce our clinic’s reliance on agency staffing?
Absolutely. Our Reclaimix program is designed to transition agency staff already working in your clinic into permanent employees, improving retention while dramatically reducing cost.
Do you staff front office or administrative roles in addition to clinical?
Yes. We place vetted support staff including receptionists, billing specialists, and schedulers who are trained to handle patient flow and keep your operations smooth.
Do you provide long-term staff or only short-term coverage?
We specialize in long-term recruitment for primary and urgent care clinics. While we can help with interim coverage when needed, our focus is on placing committed, well-matched professionals who become a lasting part of your team.
How do you make sure the candidates you recommend are right for our clinic?
We focus on matching candidates whose experience, clinical skills, and work style fit the demands of primary and urgent care. This includes verifying credentials, confirming relevant experience, and learning about your clinic’s workflow before presenting any candidates. Final hiring decisions are always yours, but our process is designed to give you strong, well-aligned options from the start.
