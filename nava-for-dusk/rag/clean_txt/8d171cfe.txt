FAQs: Payers and Insurers
Do you recruit for remote and hybrid roles?
Yes. Many payer-side functions are best suited for remote or hybrid models, and we recruit professionals with proven experience in utilization review, prior authorization, case management, and other payer functions. Candidates are evaluated for their ability to succeed in digital workflows and long-term team structures.
Can you support nationwide recruitment?
Absolutely. Our national network allows us to place professionals across multiple states while ensuring they meet CMS, NCQA, and state-specific compliance standards. We focus on long-term placements that strengthen payer teams and support consistent operations.
What is your screening process for licensed  roles?
Every candidate goes through a rigorous recruitment process that includes license verification, background checks, skills assessments, and in-depth interviews specific to payer-side knowledge. We focus on cultural and operational fit to ensure long-term retention and performance.
How does your approach differ from traditional staffing agencies?
Nava Healthcare Recruitment is not a temp or shift-based staffing agency. We specialize in long-term recruitment, connecting payers with credentialed professionals who can integrate into their teams, reduce turnover, and support sustainable growth.
