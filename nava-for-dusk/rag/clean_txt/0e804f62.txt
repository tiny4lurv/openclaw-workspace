Nava Healthcare Recruitment was founded with a goal:
To make healthcare staffing smarter, faster, and more reliable.
Our story begins not in a recruitment office, but inside the walls of a nursing home.
As former healthcare executives, we had seen firsthand the toll that bad hiring takes on facilities: open shifts, stretched staff, high turnover, and endless recruiting headaches.
The traditional recruitment model of job boards, slow follow-up, and transactional relationships just wasn’t cutting it.
Nava was built to change that.
