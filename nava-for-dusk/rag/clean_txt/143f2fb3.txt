FAQs: Long-Term Care & Assisted Living Facilities
Do you offer staffing for short-term rehab units within assisted living communities?
Yes. We provide skilled nursing staff for both long-term and short-term rehabilitation units, ensuring a seamless patient experience and consistent staffing support.
How do you help us hire staff who will stay long term?
We look beyond resumes to find candidates who are aligned with your facility’s environment, values, and day-to-day demands. Our vetting process focuses on soft skills, clinical experience, and personality fit so you’re not just hiring someone who’s qualified, but someone who’s likely to stay.
What if I’m not satisfied with a staff member?
We stand by every placement. We do not bill you unless you’re satisfied with the candidates performance post 30 days (for floor staff) of 60 days (for management positions)
