At Nava Healthcare, we specialize in community health center staffing, supporting nonprofit organizations with flexible hiring solutions and a deep understanding of your mission.
Balancing care delivery
with limited resources is never simple. Tight budgets, unpredictable patient flow, and high turnover are part of daily life all while staying focused on delivering quality care to underserved populations. Finding professionals who share your values and can contribute from day one is no small task. That’s where we come in.
Our approach is built for the realities of community-based care. From short-term support to long-term placements, we align with your clinical and operational goals without compromising what matters most: your commitment to the communities you serve.
We focus on mission-aligned professionals who understand the demands of high-need settings. Every candidate is vetted not just for their experience, but for their ability to thrive in environments where empathy, adaptability, and cultural awareness are essential. From front-desk coordinators to advanced practice providers, we staff both clinical and administrative roles with people who reflect your mission and help strengthen the care you deliver.
We support every part of your facility, from the exam room to the front desk to the broader community. Our clinical placements include physicians, nurse practitioners, physician assistants, registered nurses, LPNs, and mental health professionals. On the administrative side, we provide medical assistants, patient access coordinators, billers, and schedulers who keep your operations running smoothly. For community-facing support, we staff case managers, health workers, interpreters, and outreach coordinators who extend your care beyond the clinic walls.

FAQs: Staffing for Non-Profit & Community Health Centers
Can you help with bilingual or culturally competent staff?
Absolutely. We have recognised
multilinguism as a core skill
and routinely recruit professionals fluent in Spanish, Mandarin, Arabic, and other languages commonly needed in community settings.
How do you ensure candidates are a good fit for our facility’s mission and culture?
We take the time to understand your organization’s values, patient population, and care philosophy. Every candidate is screened not just for clinical skills, but for empathy, communication, and adaptability qualities that matter most in community-based care.
Do you offer staffing support for Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHCs)?
Yes, we understand FQHC-specific compliance and reporting requirements and staff accordingly.
