# QR-NOTEBOOK — NAVA HEALTHCARE

## WHO IS NAVA
Nava Healthcare Recruitment is a **systems partner** — not a transactional staffing vendor, but a structural partner that redesigns hiring architecture around a facility's clinical needs, operational rhythms, and long-term workforce goals. Three products: **Flagship Recruitment** (permanent placement, retention-based billing), **Reclaimix™** (converts agency nurses on the floor into permanent employees), and **NurseSphere™** (EB-3 international nurse recruitment with Schedule A processing).

## THE ONE-LINER
*"We do not fill seats; we build resilient clinical teams by acting as an integrated extension of your own leadership."*

## THE PROBLEM
Healthcare hiring was engineered for post-Great Recession surplus conditions and never rebuilt for the shortage era. The dominant "place and forget" model is optimized for placement volume, not retention — agencies collect their fee when a candidate starts; the facility absorbs every downstream cost. Reactive hiring triggers the **Backfill Spiral**: one vacancy → remaining staff cover shifts → burnout spreads → more resignations → care quality drops → revenue declines.

## FRAMEWORKS TO USE

- **Role Brief + Day Zero:** Co-created source of truth for the role (behaviors, unit context, 30/90-day success) + structured window from offer acceptance to first safe shift with preceptor 3-shift progression. Use when explaining Nava's delivery methodology — the two frameworks are inseparable.
- **Ripple Effect:** Poorly-vetted agency staff → supervisory tax on permanent staff → morale erosion → burnout → turnover → deeper agency dependence. Use to connect staffing decisions to patient outcomes and permanent staff retention.
- **QoH Scorecard:** Quality of Hire — replaces time-to-fill/cost-per-hire with Role Brief adherence, 90-day AND 1-year retention, and Day Zero candidate experience. Use when a client asks how to evaluate recruitment performance.
- **Architecture of Excellence:** Three-pillar evaluation framework for any recruitment partnership: Strategic Partnership, Disciplined Process, Clinician Candidate Experience. Use when differentiating Nava from transactional vendors.
- **Concordance Strategy:** For CHCs — hiring bilingual clinicians as a core throughput and safety mechanism, not an accommodation. *"What appears to be a language access problem is actually a workforce design problem."* Use with CHC leaders.
- **Three Buckets of Clinician Turnover:** Uncontrollable (life events), Transactional (veting failure — preventable), Systemic (Guardrail Trap — bureaucratic rigidity forcing apart willing candidates and facilities). Use when diagnosing client turnover.

## KEY TERMS

- **Day Zero:** Verbal offer acceptance → first safe shift. Four owners: HR (credentials), IT (access), unit lead (schedule/preceptor), preceptor (3-shift progression). Pre-boarding alone delivers 11% better retention.
- **Flagship Recruitment:** Retention-based billing: invoice at 30 days (floor staff), 60 days (management). Zero upfront risk; Nava's success is structurally tied to client retention.
- **Reclaimix™:** Converts agency nurses already on the floor into permanent employees. Addresses root cause of agency dependency — not finding good agency nurses, but never converting the ones you have.
- **NurseSphere™:** EB-3 green card international recruitment. 6,000-hour minimum commitment (~3 years), Schedule A processing, cohort pipelines up to 100 nurses. For building permanent embedded workforce, not 13-week coverage.
- **Schedule A Processing:** Expedited labor certification for RNs — bypasses standard PERM process. The operational mechanism that makes EB-3 viable on a facility timeline.
- **Ripple Effect:** Cascading damage from high agency utilization: supervisory tax → morale erosion → burnout → permanent staff departure → deeper agency dependence.
- **Backfill Spiral:** Reactive hiring cascade — vacancy → overtime → burnout → more departures → worse staffing → repeat.
- **Shift Shock:** 72% of new hires experience jarring disconnect between job description and day-to-day reality. Causes 20% of voluntary turnover in first 45 days.
- **Place and Forget:** Transactional model's core flaw — fills the vacancy, collects the fee, restarts the hiring cycle every 90 days.
- **Compliance-as-Checklist:** Verifying credential *existence* in a database — structurally blind to behavioral risk and fraud. Operation Nightingale (7,600+ fake diplomas) proves a valid license in a database ≠ competence.
- **Guardrail Trap:** Bureaucratic rigidity that forces apart a willing candidate and a facility that needs them. Nava calls the CFO directly to make the financial argument.
- **Lent Employee Doctrine:** Legal principle (Ruelas v. Staff Builders) — hospital bears 100% of liability for agency staff regardless of contractual provisions. Agencies are legally meaningless in court.
- **Parallel Track Strategy:** Running EB-3 and TN simultaneously — TN for urgent 2–6 month vacancies, EB-3 for permanent culture-building over 3+ years. Not competitors; complements.
- **Translation Tax:** Hidden 56% efficiency loss from interpreter model — variable cost, moral injury, clinical nuance filtered. Concordance Strategy inverts this.
- **Skin in the Game:** Nava's financial structure — revenue earned only after successful integration. Structural realignment from transaction to partnership.
